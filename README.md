# Big-Data-Insights-Myntra-Dashboard
Interactive Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard analyzing Myntra e-commerce big data (2021–2023). Features key retail metric tracking ($1.88M Total Sales, 3500 Orders), multi-dimensional slice-and-dice capability (Brand, Size, Color, Category), state-wise distribution mapping, and temporal sales trend visualizations.
 Myntra E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard:
•	Project Overview
This repository contains an interactive **Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard** that analyzes a multi-year transactional dataset from Myntra (spanning **2021 to 2023**). The dashboard transforms complex retail metrics into actionable business intelligence, focusing on brand performance, geographic order distributions, category shifts, and consumer preferences (sizes, colors, and weekdays).

•	Core KPI Metrics Achieved

1.	Total Revenue/Sales:** $1.88M 
2.	Total Order Volume:** 3,500 Orders
3.	Average Product Rating:** 4.00 / 5.00
4.	Average Markdown Discount:** 35.51%
5.	Unique Product Assortment:** 40 Products

•	Dashboard Features & Deep Dives

 1. Advanced Cross-Filtering (Slicers)
The dashboard features multi-dimensional slicing panels on the left to instantly filter the entire data canvas by:
Time Horizon:2021, 2022, 2023
Demographic Category:Women, Men, Kids, Beauty
Granular Options: Brand, Sub-category, Product Name, and Size

 2. Key Visualizations Explained
State-wise Distribution of Orders:  A stacked bar chart classifying regional demand across prominent Indian states like Gujarat, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Telangana, and Chhattisgarh.
Geographic Geospatial Map: Interactive bubble map highlighting order concentration hotspots across India.
Category Ranking By Sales: An area-stream graph showing sales volume progression across *Beauty, Kids, Men, and Women segments from 2021 to a sharp decline/taper in 2023.
Sales & Discount Trend By Month: A dual-axis combo chart tracking monthly revenue fluctuations alongside promotional discount spikes.
Sales By Weekday:An area chart pinpointing daily purchasing habits, showing a heavy acceleration in sales toward the middle and end of the week.
Granular Product Distributions: Horizontal bar charts visualizing the top-moving inventory based on specific Colors (Pink, Black, White, Off-White), Product Types (Jeans, Shorts, T-shirts), and Sizes(Onesize, 36).
